# Reach and Opportunity Playbook (90 Days)

## Positioning Guardrail

- Website core: personal writing page and interests first.
- Career outcomes: secondary layer.
- CTA voice: broad and human, not role-narrow.

## Strategy Sequence

### Phase 1 (Primary): Approach 2 - Content-Led Reach

- Publish consistently in EN/PT mixed tone.
- Focus theme: broad oncology communication.
- Reuse each post into short social formats.

### Phase 2 (After Traction): Approach 3 - Credibility Ladder

- Outreach for guest posts, talks, and collaborations.
- Convert appearances into proof assets and testimonials.

## Weekly Cadence (3-4h)

- 90 min: evidence scan and topic selection.
- 75 min: write one anchor post.
- 45 min: repurpose to one short format.
- 30 min: warm outreach and follow-up.

## Outcomes to Track

- Writing consistency (posts shipped per month).
- Relevant profile visits and meaningful replies.
- Warm conversations started.
- Referrals requested and received.

## Optional Parallel Freelance Lane

### Financial Target

- Net floor target: 300 EUR/month.
- Practical billed target with buffer: 550 EUR/month.

### Offer Stack

- Oncology evidence brief (1-2 pages).
- Slide/story narrative polish.
- Plain-language oncology explainer.

### Lead Sources

- Warm network (supervisors, collaborators, speakers).
- Medcomms agency contractor lists.
- LinkedIn targeted outreach (medical affairs and medcomms roles).

### Simple Funnel

- 40 leads/month -> 12 replies -> 4 calls -> 1-2 pilot gigs.

## Boundary Rule

Freelance and role transitions should support visibility and credibility, but not replace the personal writing identity of the website.
